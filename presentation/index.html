Features

Everything you need to
manage your fridge

Fridgely combines AI intelligence with intuitive inventory management to help you eat smarter and waste less.

💬

AI Chat Assistant

Talk to Fridgely like a friend. Ask for recipes, add items, or get suggestions — all through natural conversation powered by Claude AI.

📷

Photo Recognition

Snap a photo of your groceries and Fridgely automatically identifies and adds each item to your inventory with estimated expiry dates.

⏰

Expiry Tracking

Color-coded alerts show you what needs to be used first. Never let food go to waste with smart expiry estimation.

🍲

Smart Recipes

Get recipe suggestions that prioritize ingredients about to expire, while respecting your dietary preferences and allergies.

📋

Inventory Management

Add, remove, and track quantities with custom units. Increment and decrement with a tap. Pull-to-refresh for real-time updates.

⚙️

Personalization

Set dietary preferences, track allergies, and switch between dark and light themes. Fridgely adapts to you.

Expiry Tracking

Never waste
food again

Fridgely automatically estimates expiry dates based on food type and shows clear color-coded status indicators.

  • ✓ Red — Expired, discard or use immediately
  • ✓ Orange — Use soon, expiring today or tomorrow
  • ✓ Yellow — Expiring within 2 days
  • ✓ Green — Fresh and good to go
